**************About-Us*****************

Zala Handicrafts is a not-for-profit CSO that serves as a platform for the

promotion of tradtional handicrafts and preservation of culture, community and local environments.




Zala Handicrafts was formally registered as a Civil Society Organisation in 2015 under the CSO act of Bhutan 2012.



We aim to build an integrated, entrepreneurial and vibrant handicrafts sector in Bhutan,actively supported by international and local stakeholders that contributes increasingly to the creative, cultural and economic life of Bhutan.
