************Education and Awareness****************

raditional Bhutanese handicrafts are unique to most of the world and it is a part of Bhutan’s living culture and heritage. An increasing pressure from development and globalization observes change in people’s choice and preferences. In order to preserve and promote Bhutan’s crafts culture, Zala Handicrafts works closely with relevant national and international organizations.

Public Workshops

           

Zala Handicrafts in collaboration with committee of tourism associations and other relevant organizations invites local people, craftsperson, tour guides, tour operators, entrepreneurs and experts to join conversations about the importance of traditional crafts and its development. The space is created for providing market information and educating general public in values of traditional crafts.

Partnership with Schools


           Zala Handicrafts recognizes youth as very important 
              partner to their mission to preserve and promote
              traditional craftwork.It provides work related learning 
              experiences to the youth groups through school vocational 
              clubs in craft activities and encourage them to earn stipend
              by arranging guaranteed buyback system.
