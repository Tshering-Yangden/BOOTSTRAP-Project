About-US

At Zala.bt, our purpose is simple: To Redefine convenience! There's this notion that to grow a business, you have to be ruthless. But we know there's a better way to grow. One where what's good for the bottom line is also good for customers. We believe businesses can grow with a conscience, and succeed with a soul and that they can do it with Zala.bt. With the aim to redefine Bhutan’s shopping experience for our customers. We're creating an experience that lets customers shop anytime, anywhere - delivering exactly what they want, when and where they want it. Zala.bt is Bhutan’s pioneering e-commerce shopping destination present through physical retail store, online portal and a mobile application. Our footprint currently extends to over 79 registered stores from every part of the country with diverse product portfolio ranging from electronics, IT equipment’s, groceries, hardware tools, pet care, home appliances, baby products, fashion, Bhutan lottery and Made in Bhutan products. It all began with ABit Private limited, 6 years old, Thimphu based IT company providing complete IT solution and services to various businesses. And, Zala.bt was one of many such project the software department was working on. After a thorough market research for almost 2 years, Zala.bt was launched on July the 6th, 2020. A new e-commerce platform designed to facilitate the sale of various products online. Although we’ve learnt and grown significantly in last one year, our focus stays fixed on making life easier for our customers. It’s no secret, modern day life has time constraints as our world becomes increasingly connected and on the go. Thus, our digital team strives to be at the forefront of innovation, providing effortless and convenient solutions for our customers’ needs. We are committed to developing experiences of the future by bringing the stores to our customers wherever they are and whenever they need us. We are on a mission of innovation through software and technology enhancements. From the humble beginning as a software project, Today Zala.bt is a separate legal entity of its own and strives towards improving credibility and trust by continuing its pursuit of innovative ways to cater to a new, digital-savvy generation of shoppers. We are transforming our business, one digital initiative at a time. We are redefining convenience at Zala.bt.
